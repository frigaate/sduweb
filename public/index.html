Vision and Mission

    Our Vision:

  • To be the preferred source for the development of eminent, reliable and scalable software and Web solutions for all platforms.

    • Our Mission:

  • To develop promising entrepreneurial applications;
  • To position the university of Ibadan in the national and international software development market.
